The Juno project:

Juno is a robotic spacecraft that was launched on the 5th of August 2011, its mission is to orbit around the planet Jupiter and also collect information about how it was formed. Finally after 5 years the spaceship had entered the polar orbit of Jupiter on July 5 2016. Its mission was supposed to last 7 years but it will last longer for 12 years, 5 months and 3 days according to Nasa its mission will end of September 17, 2025.

The Apollo 11 project:

Apollo 11 was the first spaceship that landed humans on the moon on on July 20 1969 at 20:17pm. The spacecraft had 3 members Neil Alden Armstrong, Michael Collins and Edwin Eugene Aldrin Jr. Apollo 11 is the most famous mission that happened by Nasa since it proved to everyone that leaving Earth and going into space was possible.
